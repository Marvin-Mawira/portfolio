Why Choose Us

Morix Company offers expert borehole drilling services, backed by years of experience and high-quality equipment to ensure efficient and reliable water access. We provide customized solutions at competitive prices, tailored to meet the unique needs of your location.

With years of experience, our skilled team understands the complexities of drilling in diverse terrains, ensuring efficient and effective water access.

Our boreholes are built with long-term water security in mind, using sustainable practices to minimize environmental impact.

We handle everything from site analysis to drilling, pump installation, and ongoing maintenance.

Borehole Drilling

We specialize in drilling high-quality boreholes that ensure access to clean water for homes, businesses, farms, and communities. With years of experience and state-of-the-art equipment, our team provides solutions tailored to the specific needs of each project.

Solar Power Solutions

We understand the importance of a constant water supply, especially during power outages. Our solar-powered backup systems ensure your water continues flowing when electricity fails.

Clean Water Solutions

Our clean water solutions encompass a wide range of products, including filtration systems, reverse osmosis units, and water purification technologies.

Hydrogeological Survey

We use advanced techniques to assess aquifer characteristics and make data-driven decisions for borehole siting and design. Our surveys not only offer a comprehensive understanding of groundwater but also help identify potential challenges before drilling begins.

Geotechnical & Geophysical Survey

Our geotechnical and geophysical surveys help assess soil and rock properties to ensure the stability and safety of borehole construction. With expert knowledge and specialized equipment, we analyze the ground conditions to determine the best approach for your drilling project.

Environmental Impact Assessments

Our environmental impact assessments ensure that water projects comply with environmental regulations, minimizing negative impacts on the ecosystem. We evaluate potential risks and suggest mitigation measures, contributing to sustainable development and environmental conservation.

Borehole Rehabilitation

With our rehabilitation services, you can extend the lifespan of your water source and avoid costly replacements, ensuring that your system remains functional and reliable for years to come.

Water Quality Analysis

We provide water quality analysis to ensure your water source meets health and safety standards. Our comprehensive testing helps identify contaminants, ensuring your water is safe for consumption and meets regulatory standards.

Borehole Test Pumping & Design

We offer test pumping services to determine the yield and sustainability of your borehole. Our detailed analysis helps in designing the optimal pump system that guarantees consistent water supply for your needs.
